Capital One Digital for Good helps charities and non-profit organizations thrive in a digital future.

Capital One believes that charities are, and will continue to be, the foundation of a healthy community. This difficult time has pushed charities to adapt to technology at a rapid pace, and the help that we can offer through Digital for Good has never been more important.

The health and well-being of our customers, associates and communities are our top priority – that’s why we’ve made the decision to go virtual in 2020. Stay tuned for details about the event happening this November 13-15.

Digital for Good Tech Jam

November 13 - 15, 2020
Virtual Experience

The Digital for Good Tech Jam gives charities access to top tech talent. We’re on a mission to educate, empower and develop cutting-edge solutions for well-deserving charities. With a group of talented tech teams and passionate speakers, we help Canadian charities embrace and explore new technology. Every year, we rally experts from Capital One and beyond to create a powerhouse of tech capabilities. The Tech Jam pairs charities with teams from Capital One and other tech-savvy companies. Each team generously donates their skills to build a solution that amplifies their charity’s impact.

Charity Application

Applications for Digital for Good 2020 have now closed, but you can still enter your information to be considered for next year’s event.

We’re looking for Canadian registered charities that:

  • Have a technology project that they currently don’t have the capabilities to complete
  • Are able to commit a certain amount of time, staffing and resources
  • Currently use some tech and have internal resources to leverage solutions
  • Have identified a technology solution that would amplify their impact

If you’re interested in becoming a charity partner at the Digital for Good Tech Jam, please fill out an application to help us get a better understanding of your charity and your fit for the event.
